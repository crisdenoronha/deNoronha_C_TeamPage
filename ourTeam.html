DESIGNER

Cristine is a graphic designer. Her greatest interest is solving problems in visual communication for people. She likes to study user experience and interface design. When she is not working she loves to draw, try new types of coffee and desserts.

DEVELOPER

Liz is a web developer. She likes to experiment with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Liz creates sites that delight and inform. When not working, she reads books and watches science fiction films. On weekends she teaches samba to the children in her community.

brewer

Fernando began working at a local craft brewery in his hometown and immediately fell in love with the industry. He then decided to move to Canada and to do the Brewmaster Program at Niagara College (grad 2019). He makes our healthy beer delicious. When Fernando is not brewing, he spends his time playing RPG and soccer.

biochemical

Tales is the inventor of healthy beer, he won the Invention of the Year Award. He loves making new formulas and experimenting, his latest work is energy beer for the NHL. When he's not in the lab, Tales enjoys studying mythology and alchemy, where he gets his ideas for transforming beers.
